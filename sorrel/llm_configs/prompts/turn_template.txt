Step 1: Choose movement target (hare vs stag) before attacking

  - Stag: at (1,2), requires multiple moves, turns, and perhaps collaborating with allies before any hit.

  - Stag needs 4 hits total (HP=4).

  - Regen rate is 0.5 HP/turn, so a target can recover quickly once regen starts.
  - If you cannot finish before regen begins (or you get interrupted), partial damage is wasted.

4. Expected reward vs risk:

  - Hare reward is smaller (1.0) but is highly reliable and can be secured quickly.
  - In this exact state, hare has higher immediate expected reward.
